    At BigJoeFash Rafting Company, we live by the river’s rhythm. We ride with courage. Every rapid is a challenge we meet head-on. We paddle with unity. Trusting our crew, we move as one. We honor nature. Respecting the water, the land, and the life around us. We chase adventure. With strength, skill, and heart — every journey becomes a story. This is more than rafting. It’s a way of life.

    History

    BigJoeFash Rafting Company began with Jonathan “Big Joe” Apeh’s love for the river, starting as small weekend trips that grew into a trusted outfitter known for courage, camaraderie, and respect for nature. From one raft to guiding thousands through roaring rapids and serene waters, the company has built a reputation for safe, thrilling adventures that turn every journey into a lasting story.
