The SwiftStream Adventure (Beginner–Friendly)

Perfect for first-timers and families, this gentle 2-hour float follows a scenic stretch of calm river surrounded by lush greenery and wildlife. Guests enjoy light paddling, safe guided instruction, and fun splash zones ideal for photos. It’s peaceful, refreshing, and the best way to experience rafting without intense rapids.

The Rapids Rush Expedition (Intermediate Level)

This 4-hour trip takes you through a thrilling series of class II–III rapids that keep your adrenaline high but still manageable with a trained guide. Expect sharp turns, playful waves, and stretches of calm water to catch your breath. Along the way, you’ll stop at a riverside point for snacks and a short nature walk before finishing with a memorable final rapid.

The Canyon Fury Challenge (Advanced Experience)

Designed for adventure seekers, this full-day expedition tackles powerful class IV rapids in a narrow canyon known for towering cliffs and roaring water. It includes technical maneuvers, steep drops, and fast currents that demand teamwork and focus. Midway through, guests enjoy a riverside lunch before diving into the most intense section of the river.
